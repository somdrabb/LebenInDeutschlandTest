Cookie Policy

Last updated: July 4, 2025

This Cookie Policy explains how LebiDE GmbH ("we", "us", or "our") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies when you visit our website www.lebide.de.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer, smartphone, or other device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, as well as to provide information to the website owners.

We also use similar technologies such as web beacons, pixels, and local storage that may function similarly to cookies.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

Category Purpose Examples Duration
Essential Cookies Necessary for the website to function properly Session management, authentication Session or up to 24 hours
Preference Cookies Remember your choices and preferences Language selection, font size Up to 1 year
Analytics Cookies Help us understand how visitors interact Google Analytics, visitor counts Up to 2 years
Marketing Cookies Used to track visitors across websites Facebook Pixel, Google Ads Up to 1 year

3. Detailed Cookie Information

3.1 Essential Cookies

These cookies are strictly necessary to provide you with services available through our website and to use some of its features.

  • lebide_session - Maintains your login session (expires when you close your browser)
  • XSRF-TOKEN - Security cookie to prevent cross-site request forgery (expires after 24 hours)

3.2 Performance and Analytics Cookies

These cookies collect information about how visitors use our website.

  • _ga - Google Analytics cookie (expires after 2 years)
  • _gid - Google Analytics cookie (expires after 24 hours)

3.3 Functional Cookies

These cookies enable enhanced functionality and personalization.

  • language - Stores your preferred language (expires after 1 year)
  • theme - Remembers your color theme preference (expires after 1 year)

4. Managing Cookies

You can control and/or delete cookies as you wish. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually modify your browser settings to decline cookies if you prefer.

Browser Controls:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

For more information about cookies and how to manage them, visit www.allaboutcookies.org.
