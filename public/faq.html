Frequently Asked Questions

Last updated: July 2025

1. What is LebiDE?

LebiDE is a comprehensive quiz and learning platform designed to help immigrants prepare for the official "Leben in Deutschland" test. Our platform offers practice questions in multiple languages and provides state-specific (Bundesland) test preparation tailored to the regional knowledge requirements.

2. Is LebiDE officially connected to the German government?

No, LebiDE is an independent educational platform and is not affiliated with any official German government institutions. However, our question database is regularly updated to reflect the official catalog of questions used in the actual test.

3. How do I use the quizzes?

To get started:

  1. Select your preferred language
  2. Choose your Bundesland (federal state)
  3. Select from different test modes:
    • General questions (all Germany)
    • State-specific questions
    • Mixed practice test
    • Timed exam simulation

4. Is my progress saved?

Yes, when you create a free account, we automatically save:

  • Your completed tests
  • Questions you've answered incorrectly
  • Your overall progress and scores
  • Your preferred language and state settings
This allows you to track your improvement over time and focus on areas needing more practice.

5. Can I reset or delete my data?

Absolutely. You have several options:

  • Reset your progress while keeping your account
  • Delete specific test results
  • Completely delete your account and all associated data
To request data deletion, use the "Delete Account" option in your account settings or under the Legal section in the footer.

6. Is this free to use?

Yes! LebiDE is completely free to use with all features available at no cost. We believe in providing accessible preparation resources for everyone preparing for life in Germany. There are no hidden fees or premium paywalls.

7. What languages are supported?

Currently available in: German, English, Arabic, Turkish, Russian, French, Spanish, and Farsi. We're continuously adding more language options based on user demand.

8. How often are questions updated?

We update our question database quarterly to reflect any changes in the official test catalog. You'll receive a notification when new questions are available if you've enabled updates in your account settings.
